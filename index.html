Hybrid Inverters (1.5KW - 12KW, Single-phase & 3-phase)

All our inverters can be monitored remotely, are hybrid (designed with inbuilt solar charge controller), and can be scaled up by 6units (except for the non-parallel models). They are also compatible with tubular batteries and lithium batteries of other brands. They all come with a 2year warranty, except the HESP model that comes with a 5year warranty. You can use them off-grid or together with the grid.
